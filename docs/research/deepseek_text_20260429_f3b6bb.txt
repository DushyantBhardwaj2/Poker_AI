
### Module-wise Architecture
1. **Game Engine** – Manages players, stack sizes, blinds, pot, side pots, and rounds.
2. **Action Tracker** – Validates and records every action (bet, raise, fold, etc.) using an event-sourcing model.
3. **Probability Engine** – Calculates winning percentages via Monte Carlo simulations.
4. **AI Engine** – Serves opponent classification, bluff detection, and move recommendations.
5. **Hand History Analyzer** – Stores completed hands and computes aggregated player statistics.
